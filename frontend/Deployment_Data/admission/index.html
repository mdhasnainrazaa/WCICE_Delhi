Basic Eligibility

Academic Qualification

  • 10th & 12th Standard Complete
  • General: Min 50% in PCB
  • Reserved: Min 40% in PCB

NEET Qualification

NEET qualification is mandatory for Indian students who want to pursue MBBS abroad and practice medicine in India in the future.

Age Requirement

Students must be at least 17 years old at the time of admission.

Documents Required

Academic

  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet
  • Leaving Certificate
  • Migration Certificate

Identity

  • PAN Card (Optional)
  • NEET Scorecard
