Why Do You Need a Professional MBBS Abroad Consultancy?

Applying for a medical seat outside of India involves several high-stakes procedures, including government university verifications, foreign ministry legalizations, apostille translation certificates, and dynamic visa embassy updates. A single error in the registration files can lead to admission rejection or visa denial.

By partnering with a trusted and direct **MBBS abroad consultant in Delhi** like WCIEC Delhi, you ensure a safe and professional route. We eliminate intermediaries and fake sub-agents, guaranteeing direct university allocations, transparent fee structures, and immediate hostel clearances.

How WCIEC Delhi Helps Aspiring Medical Students

WCIEC Delhi has established its reputation as a leading education consulting firm by offering comprehensive, end-to-end guidance under one single roof:

1. University Selection & Profiling

We analyze your NEET scores, academic credentials, and financial budget to match you with the absolute best gov/private medical university abroad.

2. Official Application Processing

Our Delhi team handles complete form submissions, document translations, university registrations, and gathers official ministry admission letters.

3. Ministry Invitation & Visa

We handle the entire documentation, apostille processing, bank statements review, medical fitness logs, and passport visa stamping directly at the embassies.

4. Pre-Departure Briefings

A comprehensive seminar hosted in Delhi for students and parents regarding local laws, transit protocols, university hostels, and currency exchange.

5. Active Airport Assistance

A WCIEC team coordinator travels physically with the student group from Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGI) Delhi to Kyrgyzstan campuses.

6. Direct On-Ground Support

Our on-ground staff manages hostel registration, local sim card setups, local health check-ups, and student support for the entire 5 years.

Parental Counselling & Safe Payment Protocols

  • Direct Bank Deposits: Students deposit all tuition fees directly into the official bank accounts of the foreign universities, preventing cash handling by third parties.
