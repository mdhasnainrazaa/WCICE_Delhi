Overview of MBBS in Russia

Russia has maintained its status as a highly prestigious destination for medical education over several decades. Pursuing MBBS in Russia for Indian students is highly sought after due to its century-old government medical universities, advanced infrastructure, and globally recognized curriculum.

When you choose to study medicine abroad in Russia, you are awarded an MD Physician degree, which is strictly equivalent to the MBBS degree in India. Indian students choose Russia because it provides a perfect blend of high-quality European medical education, immense clinical exposure at massive state hospitals, and highly affordable tuition fees compared to private Indian institutions.

Is MBBS in Russia Valid in India?

Yes, MBBS degrees from recognized Russian medical universities are entirely valid in India, provided they align with the latest NMC guidelines for foreign medical graduates.

  • Course Duration: Russia strictly follows a 6-year duration (54 months minimum theoretical + practical study).
  • Language: The entire course must be conducted in English medium.
  • Internship Requirements: A mandatory 1-year internship must be completed in the same university.
  • Licensing: Indian students must clear the NExT/FMGE licensing process to practice locally.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is NEET mandatory for MBBS in Russia?

Yes, qualifying the NEET-UG exam is strictly mandatory for Indian students to study MBBS in Russia and later practice medicine in India.

What is the duration of MBBS in Russia?

The total duration of the MBBS (MD Physician) program in Russia is 6 years, which includes 5 years of theoretical/clinical study and 1 year of mandatory internship.

Is MBBS in Russia taught in English?

Yes, top NMC approved universities in Russia offer 100% English medium medical courses specially designed for international and Indian students.

What is the total cost of MBBS in Russia?

The total cost of studying MBBS in Russia ranges from INR 20 Lakhs to 50 Lakhs depending on the university, city, and lifestyle. This includes tuition fees and hostel accommodation.

Are Russian medical universities approved by NMC?

Yes, many renowned Russian medical universities are fully recognized by the WHO and strictly comply with the latest NMC guidelines for foreign medical graduates.

Can Indian students practice after MBBS from Russia?

Absolutely. After completing their MBBS degree in Russia, Indian students can practice in India by qualifying the NExT/FMGE licensing examination.

Which is the cheapest MBBS university in Russia?

Bashkir State Medical University and Far Eastern Federal University are among the more affordable options, offering high-quality education starting at around INR 20-25 Lakhs for the total course.
