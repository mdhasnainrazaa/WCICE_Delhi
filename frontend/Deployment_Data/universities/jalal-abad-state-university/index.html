About Jalal-Abad State University

Jalal-Abad State University (JASU), named after B. Osmonov, is one of the leading medical universities in Kyrgyzstan established in 1993. The university provides globally recognized MBBS education with modern infrastructure and advanced clinical training.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is JASU NMC approved?

Yes, Jalal-Abad State University is fully recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and WHO, making the degree valid for practice in India after passing NExT/FMGE.

What is the total MBBS fees?

The total fee for the entire 6-year program is approximately ₹34.69 Lakhs, which covers tuition, hostel, and initial processing.

Is Indian food available?

Yes, the university has a dedicated Indian mess on campus serving North and South Indian food prepared by Indian chefs.

Is NEET required?

Yes, qualifying NEET-UG is mandatory for all Indian students who wish to study MBBS abroad and later practice in India.

What is the course duration?

The duration is 5 years of academic study followed by a 1-year mandatory clinical internship (5+1 years).
