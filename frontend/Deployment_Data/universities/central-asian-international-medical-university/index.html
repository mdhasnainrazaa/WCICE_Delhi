About the University

Central Asian International Medical University (CAIMU) is widely recognized as the most affordable yet highly innovative medical university in Kyrgyzstan. It prepares Indian students to excel in global healthcare.

Established in 2018, CAIMU was developed from the ground up with a singular mission: to provide the highest quality, competency-based medical education at the lowest possible cost. CAIMU features modern, bright lecture halls, extensive anatomical museums, and a highly dedicated faculty that works closely with international students. The university has quickly become a top choice for students looking for an incredible return on investment without compromising on NMC standards.

Clinical Exposure & Attached Hospitals

CAIMU operates with a direct focus on early patient interaction. The university has forged strong alliances with major regional hospitals in Jalal-Abad. Indian students are guided through intensive clinical rotations, ensuring they are well-versed in practical diagnostics and patient care.

FMGE / NExT Preparation & Performance

CAIMU places a massive emphasis on the FMGE/NExT exam. The academic board frequently reviews and adjusts the curriculum to perfectly align with Indian medical standards, resulting in excellent pass rates for hardworking students.

Key Advantages

  • •One of the most cost-effective tuition fee structures in Kyrgyzstan.
  • •Curriculum is perfectly tailored for Indian medical students.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q.Is CAIMU NMC Approved?

Yes, CAIMU NMC Approved status is fully confirmed. It complies strictly with all National Medical Commission guidelines for Indian students.

Q.What are the CAIMU Fees?

CAIMU Fees are highly affordable, offering incredible value with tuition set at just $3,600 per year.

Q.How is the Central Asian International Medical University Hostel?

The Central Asian International Medical University Hostel is highly secure, clean, and provides a peaceful, study-focused environment for Indian students.

Q.How to get CAIMU Admission?

CAIMU Admission is straightforward and transparent via WCIEC Delhi. Submit your NEET card and 12th marks to get guaranteed admission.

Q.What is the CAIMU Ranking?

The CAIMU Ranking is highly competitive, positioned as a top-tier private medical college for international students in Central Asia.

Q.Is CAIMU MBBS good for Indian students?

Absolutely. Studying CAIMU MBBS offers modern education, great hostel facilities, excellent NExT coaching, and high campus safety.
