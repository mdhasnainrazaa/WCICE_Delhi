Overview

Central Asian International Medical University (CAIMU): Overview

Central Asian International Medical University (CAIMU) is widely recognized as the most affordable yet highly innovative medical university in Kyrgyzstan. It prepares Indian students to excel in global healthcare.

Established in 2020, CAIMU was developed from the ground up with a singular mission: to provide the highest quality, competency-based medical education at the lowest possible cost. CAIMU features modern, bright lecture halls, extensive anatomical museums, and a highly dedicated faculty that works closely with international students. The university has quickly become a top choice for students looking for an incredible return on investment without compromising on NMC standards.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must have scored a minimum of 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in their 12th grade.

They should also be at least 17 years old by December 31st of the admission year.

The NEET UG test must be qualified for admission.

Courses & Duration

Students begin with a 5-year MBBS program supervised by knowledgeable and experienced instructors. Upon completing the course, they are required to undergo a 1-year internship, obtaining practical experience by caring for patients in medical facilities.
