Frequently Asked Questions

Do foreign medical graduates need to clear a licensing exam in India?

Yes. Foreign medical graduates must follow the applicable Indian licensing pathway, currently involving FMGE/NExT-related requirements as notified by authorities.

Should FMGE/NExT affect university choice?

Yes. Clinical exposure, teaching language, faculty support, and exam preparation should be part of the university decision.

Does WCIEC provide FMGE/NExT guidance?

WCIEC should position its university counseling around licensing readiness, clinical exposure, and exam-support ecosystem.
