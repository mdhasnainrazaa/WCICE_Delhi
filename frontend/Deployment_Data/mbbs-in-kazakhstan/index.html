Is MBBS in Kazakhstan Valid in India?

We understand that parents and students are deeply concerned about degree validity. Rest assured, your medical degree from Kazakhstan is fully valid in India, provided the university meets the strict NMC regulations:

  • Course Duration: Must meet the minimum 54 months (4.5 years) threshold. Kazakhstan offers a secure 5-year program.
  • Language: The entire course must be in the English medium, which is guaranteed.
  • Internship Requirement: You must complete a 12-month clinical internship.
  • Licensing Exam: Graduates are fully eligible to appear for the NExT/FMGE licensing exams to practice safely in India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kazakhstan good for MBBS for Indian students?

Yes, Kazakhstan is considered an excellent destination for Indian students due to its highly affordable fees, modern medical infrastructure, entirely English-taught curriculum, and safe international environment.

Is NEET compulsory for MBBS in Kazakhstan?

Yes. Qualifying the NEET-UG examination is strictly mandatory for Indian students planning to pursue MBBS abroad and seeking to practice medicine back in India.

What is the duration of MBBS in Kazakhstan?

The duration of the MBBS program in Kazakhstan is 5 years, which complies with the NMC's requirement for foreign medical graduates (minimum 54 months).

What is the fees of MBBS in Kazakhstan?

The total estimated cost for MBBS in Kazakhstan ranges between INR 20 Lakhs to 25 Lakhs. This generally includes the total 5-year tuition fee and hostel accommodation.

Are Kazakhstan medical universities approved by NMC?

Yes, many of the top medical universities in Kazakhstan are fully recognized by WHO, FAIMER, and comply with the latest NMC guidelines.

Is MBBS in Kazakhstan taught in English?

Yes, all the major universities offering medical programs for international students provide 100% English medium medical education for the entire 5 years.

Does WCIEC help with Kazakhstan visa processing?

Absolutely. WCIEC Delhi provides end-to-end support, from university application to complete visa processing, document apostille, and final departure assistance.
