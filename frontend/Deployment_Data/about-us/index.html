Who We Are

Trusted by 5000+ Future Doctors

WCIEC Organization was established with a clear vision: to guide aspiring Indian medical students toward world-class MBBS opportunities abroad — particularly in the Kyrgyz Republic. Over the years, we've built a strong reputation for transparent admissions support, genuine student care, and consistently outstanding FMGE/NExT results.

Today, WCIEC proudly owns and operates 5 medical universities in Jalalabad and Osh — all recognized by the NMC and WHO — and was honored with the Best University Award in Kyrgyzstan and awarded by the President of Kyrgyzstan.
