Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best university in Kyrgyzstan for Indian medical students?

Osh State University and Jalal Abad State University are government-recognized universities and are highly recommended. Osh State is the oldest medical faculty in the country and has the largest clinical hospitals, whereas Jalal-Abad State offers premium academic infra and highly experienced professors.

What is the medium of teaching in Kyrgyzstan medical universities?

The medium of instruction for all foreign students is 100% English. WCIEC Delhi represents and advises ONLY those government-recognized universities that conduct all academic lectures, labs, and exams in English.

What is the difference between a government university and private colleges?

Government universities in Kyrgyzstan are state-subsidized, have over 50 years of academic research budgets, maintain massive clinical hospitals, and offer historic alumni networks. Private universities are modern, offering advanced simulated anatomical laboratories and smaller class sizes.

How do I secure an education loan for these universities?

WCIEC Delhi provides students with certified university admission letters, bonafide certificates, embassy legal invites, and official breakdown structures of tuition and hostel fees to help parents secure education loans from all major Indian banks.
